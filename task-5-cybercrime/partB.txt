Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist 
General Safety Tips 
• Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages.  
• Always check the sender’s email address carefully.  
• Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts.  
• Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.  
• Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.  
• Keep your devices and apps updated regularly.  
UPI / Financial Safety Tips 
• Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.  
• Do not approve “collect requests” unless you trust the sender.  
• Double-check payment requests before accepting them.  
• Use only official apps like BHIM, Google Pay, or PhonePe.  
Reporting Cybercrime 
• Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
• Call Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
